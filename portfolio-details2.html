About

In this project I have performed exploratory data analysis over the stackoverflow developer dataset for the year 2018 and 2019. The objective is to analyse developers trends for the year 2020. I have used python programming libraies and its powerful visualizations.

  • Tech Stack: Python, pandas, numpy, plotly

    • Data

    The data used here is from the survey done by stack-overflow every year. It contains responses of approximately 1 lac entries with a wide range of questions.

    To access the data Click here

    Visualizations

    Based upon the data I have performed following tasks to complete our comparable study,

  • Year on Year comparision of total developers for each country



  • Sunburst Representation for 2018

    • Hierarichal representation of 2018 developers for top 5 countries. First layer: Country, Second layer : Types of Developers, Third layer: Gender.




  • Sunburst Representation for 2019

    • Hierarichal representation of 2019 developers for top 5 countries. First layer: Country, Second layer : Types of Developers, Third layer: Gender.
